About

Illustrator Transitioning into Web Development

At 8 years old, I joined a website called Neopets and made two life-long friends as well as a love for creating webpages.

Since then, I've been playing with making websites as a hobby.

Some websites I have worked on include Philippine Spirits, a site based on Philippine mythology and folklore. Another is ComicFighter, which a is a large database of more than 700 comics translated from Japanese to English mainly about fighting games.
