Bisaya Island Characters Lore OOC

Bisaya Island

The island houses a giant balete tree, similar to strangling figs

It's a dwelling space for nature spirits and supernatural creatures.

Physically, the entrance to Bisaya Island is located in the Far East.

However, Bisaya Island thrives in a world parallel to The Source.

You won't find species like Hyurs or Miqo'te here.

Instead, you'll find...

Civets

It's easy to confuse a civet with a Miqo'te.

Tamawo

These are similar beings to Moogles or fairies.

They can shapeshift and turn invisible.

Depending on the tamawo's mood, you may find flowers or rocks being thrown at you.

If somebody makes a tamawo upset, the person can get very sick.

Aswang

An aswang's favorite meal is a corpse.

Looking into their mouths, you may find a red thread in place of their tongue.

They can separate from their torsos and fly around.

Gifted in magic, an aswang can create magic oils with properties that may cause someone to fly.

Characters

As the balete tree of Bisaya Island began to rot, a group of individuals traveled to the Source to find a cure.


Tang Moon

An affable civet whose good will seems to have strings attached.

Sanzang Sun

Also known as Sunny.

A sheltered tamawo who hates bullying.

She is Tang's little sister!

Tian Peng

A tamawo who is naturally mischievous and trying to set her son up on dates.

She's Sunny's best friend!

Fortunata Peng

Also known as Tuna.

An aswang who chooses to make an honest living by selling bath and beauty products.

She's Tian's wife!
